About Us

Tree Story is India's leading and most trusted online plant nursery, dedicated to fulfilling all your gardening needs. Our online nursery offers an extensive range of high-quality exotic indoor plants, outdoor plants, flowering plants, and succulents. Additionally, Tree Story specializes in organic vegetable, herb, and flower seeds. We also provide trendy, premium planters, top-notch soil and fertilizers, and a comprehensive selection of gardening tools essential for every home gardener.

At Tree Story, we believe we are more than just a garden store; we are your partner in your gardening journey. We understand that plants help people grow, and we are committed to nurturing this growth. We embrace the magic of cultivating plants, discovering our inner selves in the process, and cherishing moments like your first new leaf, your first bloom, or your first sprouted seed.

Tree Story takes pride in being more than just an online garden store or plant nursery. We are your trusted friend, dedicated to helping both you and your garden thrive. With our lifetime gardening support, we are here to guide you through all your gardening adventures.
